About Us

Welcome to Reed – Your Destination for Beauty & Wellness

Reed Beauty and Fitness Center was founded by three friends who share a deep passion for wellness and beauty. United by a vision to create a space that nurtures both inner and outer well-being, we combined our expertise to bring Reed to life. Our journey began with a simple goal: to offer a sanctuary where self-care, fitness, and beauty come together in harmony.

We believe in holistic well-being—where movement, mindfulness, and self-care rituals empower individuals to look and feel their best. Whether through yoga, fitness, or beauty treatments, our mission is to provide a transformative experience that enhances confidence, relaxation, and overall health.

Join us on this journey, and let Reed be your destination for balance, beauty, and well-being.

Our Story –
The Journey of Reed Salon

Reed Salon was born from a passion for beauty, creativity, and self-care. With years of experience in the industry, our founders envisioned a space where elegance meets innovation. Our salon was created to be more than just a place for hair and beauty—it’s a retreat where clients can unwind, rejuvenate, and leave feeling transformed.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a go-to destination for beauty lovers, REED has always prioritized quality, client satisfaction, and staying ahead of the latest trends.

To inspire a holistic approach to well-being by offering high-quality beauty and fitness services that nurture both the body and mind.

Wellness & Balance – Encouraging a harmonious lifestyle through movement, mindfulness, and self-care.

Quality & Expertise – Providing professional, high-standard services in beauty and fitness.

Community & Empowerment – Creating a welcoming space where individuals feel supported on their journey to self-improvement.

To become a leading destination in Abu Dhabi for beauty and fitness, where individuals can enhance their well-being through expert-led services in a serene and inspiring environment.
